Verify your account

NTU Pair is available to every user on the internet, including scammers and impostors. To provide a safe environment for students to match and communicate, we recommend verifying your account using NTU Webmail system, G-Suite, or custom email services if you don't have an NTU id.

Due to the school policies and the Webmail spam filtering algorithm, our verification email might be intercepted and sent to the spam folder. Thus, it is more recommended to use G-Suite for verification. If you use Webmail to verify your account but fail to recieve a verification email, it means our email is intercepted on the way. Please go to the spam folder on https://mfilter.ntu.edu.tw:83/ to retrieve the mail or wait for the "Spam Quarantine Notification" to view the mail.
