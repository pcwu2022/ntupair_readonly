NTU Pair

Welcome to NTU Pair, a platform designed to couple up NTU students who share the same value or have similar interests!

NTU is vast! With over 30,000 students enrolled, making friends with people all around the campus is pretty difficult, not to mention finding the most suitible one. While finding the perfect match merely by chance is never a good idea, NTU Pair helps you find those with traits similar to you by using our paring algorithm. Our seek for the perfect match begins by answering several questions. By doing the tests, our system can collect information about your personality and use these data to find those with similar traits. After filling out the forms, you can choose which data to be used in the matching process, and which types of data to be selected from the database. With the integration of our chatbox interface, our system provides instant messaging service for you to chat with those on your match list. You can make message requests, approve or decline others' requests on your free will. Test, match, chat, and get engaged, NTU Pair is always the best choice for you.

NTU Pair is a free and secure platform for you to acquaint others and make connections with whom you're mindsets are similar to. Your password and messaging history are encrypted during transfer and in the database, and your student id as well as email address are immediately discarded after essential use. We provide an email verification system to filter out dummy accounts and ensure the safety of our verified users in NTU. However, it is not recommended to disclose personal information without proper reasons, for the information might be misused by other users. To report errors or for custom service, please contact ntupair@gmail.com.
